General Information

As the Faculty Advisor for Pi Sigma Alpha, the National Political Science Honor Society at CSUF, I have the privilege of working with a talented group of students dedicated to excelling in the field of political science, in education, and in thier careers. This society recognizes and promotes high academic achievement in political science, providing our members with unique opportunities for scholarly growth, networking, and leadership development.

Our activities range from discussions and guest lectures to community service initiatives and networking events. We assist with the annual Royce Symposium, the University Town Hall, and other campus actvites. Our annual induction banquet is each spring semester.

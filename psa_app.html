Epsilon-Eta Chapter

Requirements

Undergraduate Students:

  • Minimum 3.0 cumulative GPA
  • Minimum 3.25 POSC GPA
  • Completion of ten units in political science
  • At least three upper-division units (300-level or higher)
  • Open to political science majors and minors

Graduate Students:

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Completion of nine graduate units

$75 One-Time Membership Fee:

  • This fee covers lifetime membership, including national dues, chapter dues, honor cords, and a medallion
  • Check or Cash is accepted. Please deliver to GH 516 or mailbox in GH 511
  • Dues must be paid before the application is processed

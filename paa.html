General Information

As the Faculty Advisor for Pi Alpha Alpha, the Global Honor Society for Public Affairs & Administration at CSUF, I work with a diverse cadre of high-performing students dedicated to excellence in public administration. These students are excelling academically and making substantial strides in their respective careers. The society is dedicated to honoring and fostering high academic accomplishments in the sphere of public administration, creating avenues for intellectual development, professional networking, and leadership growth.

We engage in a wide array of activities, including insightful discussions, lectures from guest speakers, community service initiatives, and professional networking events. The society's hallmark event is the annual induction banquet held each spring semester.

About Pi Alpha Alpha

Joining Pi Alpha Alpha is more than just an acknowledgment of academic success. It's an identification with a group of individuals committed to public affairs and administration. Members gain access to unique service opportunities organized by individual Pi Alpha Alpha chapters, allowing them to practically apply their classroom knowledge in local community contexts.

In addition to community involvement, members gain access to a vast network of peers and professionals in public service both domestically and globally. They can connect with this network through various platforms, including the Global Society's Facebook group. Moreover, membership in Pi Alpha Alpha is a testament to a candidate's qualifications and potential, signaling to prospective employers the member's exceptional capabilities.

Pi Alpha Alpha provides members the chance to visually celebrate their academic accomplishments by purchasing honor cords, medallions, and other such items, signifying their society affiliation.

I serve as a faculty member in the Division of Politics, Administration & Justice at Cal State Fullerton, I teach courses in public policy, public administration, and collaborative governance. My research focuses on the confluence of public policy, community involvement, and environmental justice, paying special attention to the results of collaborative governance in the realm of water policy.

Before joining Cal State Fullerton, I earned my Ph.D. in Public Policy and Public Administration from Auburn University. Outside of my professional pursuits, I cherish the time spent with my children and pets, both indoors and out. I try to soak up and embrace the rich diversity of Southern California's people, landscapes, and culinary scenes. And I like to volunteer for various orgaizations and student clubs. I also have a deep connection with the people and authors from home, many of whom tell thier stories to The Bitter Southerner.
